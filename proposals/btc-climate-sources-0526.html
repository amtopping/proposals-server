BlackTech Capital — Research Reference

International Climate Tech Sources

Firsthand and practitioner-level sources by geography — not US-centric aggregate reporting

Honest note on China: Chinese climate tech investors writing their own perspective in English the way a US or UK practitioner would does not yet exist as a format. Chinese investment culture runs through WeChat, closed ecosystems, and state-aligned institutions. The sources below are insider-staffed and primary-source oriented. The gap is real — direct relationships are not substitutable by any newsletter.
Source Directory
Geography Source What It Actually Is Link
China TechNode English-language tech journalism written by people inside China's ecosystem. Covers EV, clean energy, and climate policy from a Chinese market lens — not foreign correspondents. technode.com
China New Energy Nexus China Practitioners running clean energy accelerators in Shanghai. Closest thing to an insider operator blog in English on China's cleantech ecosystem. newenergynexus.com
China Carbon Brief China Briefing Fortnightly newsletter compiled by China-based researchers. Sourced directly from Chinese regulators, state media, and research institutions. Not foreign aggregation. Free. carbonbrief.org/newsletter
China China Energy Policy Newsletter Published by China's Energy Research Institute. Actual policy insiders writing in English monthly. Subscribe by emailing china@ens.dk cet.energy
China Caixin Global English-language outlet owned and staffed by Chinese journalists. The Wall Street Journal equivalent for Chinese financial and economic reporting — primary source, not translated analysis. caixinglobal.com
China / Global China Cleantech Podcast Hosted by Andrew Chang (New Energy Nexus China) and Marilyn Waite (Climate Finance Fund). Best English-language audio on China climate investment from practitioners inside the market. chinacleantech.co
India Avaana Capital India's first climate VC fund. They publish and podcast directly from the field — founders, investors, and policymakers across India's energy transition. avaana.in
India Climate Money Substack Investor-perspective coverage of India and Asia-Pacific climate VC with deal-level analysis. More analytical than aggregate. climatemoney.substack.com
Africa / Nigeria Africa: The Big Deal Two practitioners tracking every startup funding deal on the continent with their own proprietary data. Climate tech was 32% of all African startup funding in 2024 per their numbers. thebigdeal.substack.com
Africa / Nigeria The Green Techpreneur London-based but Africa-focused in practice. Publishes firsthand African founder and investor deep dives with unit economics and direct quotes — not aggregate reporting. thegreentechpreneur.substack.com
Africa Afridigest Covers Africa tech investment including climate from an investor and operator lens. Deal-level tracking with practitioner context. afridigest.substack.com
Africa Climate Hack Global Published a 40+ African climate tech investor directory with fund details and thesis summaries. Good for mapping the ecosystem quickly. news.climatehack.global
London / Europe IIGCC Newsletter Institutional Investors Group on Climate Change. European institutional investors writing their own policy positions — not reporting on investors, written by them. Monthly, free. iigcc.org
London / Europe LSEG Sustainable Growth Podcast London Stock Exchange Group. Guests include BlackRock, First Abu Dhabi Bank, ISSB. Corporate Europe's actual voice on climate investment — not analysis of it. lseg.com/podcasts
Singapore / Asia Tech in Asia Largest English-language tech media in Asia. Covers climate deals across Southeast Asia and China from inside the region — staffed locally, not by foreign correspondents. techinasia.com

Carbon Brief China Briefing is the best free newsletter for staying current week to week. None of these replace direct investor relationships — they supplement them.
