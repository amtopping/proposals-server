What the grid is telling you

The column that pays the rent today — Founders — was not built. It arrived warm, through your network, and skipped every rung straight to high-ticket. Its attraction and core cells are empty on purpose: that hollow space is real.

The tidy attraction-to-core walkthroughs on the right are for buyers who aren’t paying yet. The educator column is the one bright spot — it has a real, converting core rung in the foot-in-the-door workshop, which is why it’s the closest thing you have to a reproducible path.

So the gap isn’t conversion. It’s that your proven, highest-value buyer has no reproducible path beneath it — which is exactly why volume depends on who already knows you instead of on a system. The build worth doing is the founders walkthrough: an attraction and core rung that could land a fractional engagement without needing a prior relationship.
