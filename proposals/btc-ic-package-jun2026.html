Investment Committee: Role & Decision Framework

As members of the BlackTech Capital Investment Committee, your responsibility is to rigorously evaluate investment opportunities against two core questions:

🎯 Question 1: Thesis Alignment

Does this opportunity align with BTC's promised investment thesis?

CleanTech/ClimateTech solution + Underrepresented founder + Venture-scale market potential

💪 Question 2: Investment Quality

Is this a solid and respectable investment opportunity?

Strong fundamentals, defensible market position, credible execution path, founder quality

Your IC Responsibilities

  • Evaluate consistency with Fund thesis, stage, geography, and risk profile
  • Provide sectoral, technical, and operational insight
  • Challenge assumptions and identify potential risks
  • Conduct additional research or diligence as requested
  • Contribute informed recommendations: INVEST / PASS / FURTHER DILIGENCE

Note: The IC recommendation is advisory. Final investment decision authority rests exclusively with the General Partners, pursuant to the Fund's Limited Partnership Agreement.

Evaluation Criteria & Framework

Each opportunity is evaluated on the following weighted criteria to ensure rigorous, consistent analysis:
